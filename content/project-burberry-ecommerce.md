## Overview

At EPAM Systems, I contributed to Burberry's e-commerce web application, a customer-facing platform that supported the brand's online retail experience. My work focused on frontend engineering and performance improvement, with an emphasis on building a fast, polished, and reliable experience for users.

## Frontend and Performance Work

I worked on frontend improvements that helped enhance the overall responsiveness and efficiency of the application. This included optimizing how frontend assets were delivered, improving rendering performance, and supporting a more scalable server-side setup for the web application.

I also contributed to media delivery improvements by applying responsive image techniques, helping ensure that users received appropriately sized assets for different devices and viewing conditions. These efforts supported better loading behavior and a smoother browsing experience.

## Rendering and User Experience

Part of my work involved improving server-side rendering to support both user experience and search visibility. This helped strengthen the initial page experience for content-heavy commerce pages and contributed to better overall frontend performance.

## Team Contribution

Beyond product delivery, I contributed to team development through onboarding support, knowledge sharing, and technical interviews. I also delivered internal JavaScript Fundamentals lessons, helping colleagues strengthen core language knowledge and frontend development practices.

## Why This Experience Matters

This experience was meaningful because it combined hands-on product work with broader engineering impact. It was not only about delivering features, but also about improving performance, maintainability, and team capability. It reflects the kind of work I enjoy most: building user-focused web experiences while contributing to a stronger engineering culture.

## Highlights

- Contributed to frontend improvements for a global e-commerce experience
- Supported performance optimization across rendering and asset delivery
- Applied responsive image techniques to improve media loading efficiency
- Helped strengthen engineering practices through onboarding, teaching, and interviews
