## Context

I led the frontend architecture for a rate automation system in Expedia PartnerCentral. The product area focused on helping partners manage pricing workflows more effectively inside a large-scale platform serving Expedia's global hotel partners.

## What Happened

This initiative required balancing product flexibility, architecture consistency, and long-term maintainability. As a technical lead, I worked across implementation details and broader system direction while partnering closely with PMs and UX designers.

## Planned Expansion

The final version will expand on the product problem, the main frontend architecture decisions, the launch impact, and lessons from coordinating across product, UX, and engineering teams.

## Why This Matters

This was one of the clearest examples of combining technical leadership with product thinking. It was not only about writing code, but also about shaping direction, mentoring others, and keeping the system scalable as the initiative grew.
